I am an Electrical Engineer and Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Neuroimaging Research Branch, NIDA-IRP, NIH. Before, I was a graduate student at Experimental Mechanics and Computer Vision (EMCV) Labs, Catholic University of America where I worked under the supervision of Dr. Zhaoyang Wang on 3D imaging systems. Meanwhile, I received B.E. and M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering Department, Catholic University of America.

Research Interest

  • Real-time, high-accuracy 3D imaging and shape measurement
  • Camera and depth sensors
  • Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) control
  • Neuroimaging Modalities
